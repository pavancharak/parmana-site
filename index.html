The Real Problem
The problem is not that AI makes decisions.

The real problem begins when AI becomes the source of the authority that governs people, organizations, and critical systems.

Authority must remain human.
Humans establish the authority, define the boundaries, and determine the policies within which AI operates. This responsibility cannot be delegated.
