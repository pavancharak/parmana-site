Human Authority for AI Execution

Parmana ensures there is no gap between what humans decide and what AI systems do.

As AI systems gain the ability to take actions, organizations need confidence that those actions match approved decisions. Parmana makes that possible.

See How It Works View Demo

The Problem

Decisions are tracked. Actions are not.

Organizations can usually see who approved a decision and when it was approved. What is often missing is proof that AI actually did what was approved.

Closing the Gap

A direct link between human decisions and AI actions.

Parmana creates a connection between what an organization approves and what its AI systems actually carry out so nothing happens without a corresponding decision.

  • Keep AI actions aligned with human decisions
  • Prevent unauthorized actions before they happen
  • Verify what was approved and what was executed
  • Create confidence in AI-powered operations

Core Principle

AI may be unpredictable. Execution should not be.

AI can generate many possible actions. Parmana helps ensure that only approved actions are executed so organizations can confidently deploy AI without losing control of what it does.

How It Works

Five steps, no surprises.

01

Humans make a decision

A person or team defines what the AI is allowed to do and under what conditions.

02

The intended action is recorded

Parmana captures the approved action before anything is executed.

03

AI attempts execution

The AI system moves to carry out the action in the real world.

04

Parmana verifies the action

Before anything happens, Parmana checks whether the action matches what was approved.

05

Approved actions execute

If it matches, the action proceeds. If it doesn't, it is stopped. A verifiable record is created either way.
